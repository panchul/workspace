## Images

     Inline-style: 
    ![alt text](URI_TO_IMAGE "Image title text")
    
    Reference-style: 
    ![alt text][some_icon]
    
    [some_icon]: URI_TO_IMAGE "Image title text"
 
Inline-style: 
![alt text](URI_TO_IMAGE "Image title text")

Reference-style: 
![alt text][some_icon]

[some_icon]: URI_TO_IMAGE "Image title text"

To scale, you can replace it with html-like format:

    ![mypic.png](mypic.png)

to

    <img src="mypic.png" width="40%" height="40%">
