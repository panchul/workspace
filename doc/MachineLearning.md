Timelines of Machine Learning:

1943 - Neural Networks
1957-62 - Perceptron
1970-86 - Backpropagation, RBM, RNN
1979-98 - CNN, MNIST, LSTM, Bidirectional RNN
2006 - "Deep learning", DBN
2014 - GANs
2016-17 - AlphaGo, AlphaZero
2017-19 - Transformers
